Department of Surgery

Most advanced and fully equipped procedure rooms for performing surgeries.

From early age de-sexing to injuries in later years, surgeries are sometimes required to make life easier for your pets. In some cases, these are inevitable. At Pet Trust, we have experienced surgeons who can administer surgical procedures on your pets from desexing to complicated surgeries. The surgeries in pets always have the risk of using anesthesia. But, the chances of recovery are also high after surgeries.

Department of Veterinary Medicine

Our veterinarians, para veterinary staff and technicians are ever ready and equipped to give any medical care and assistance that your pet needs.

Pets are family members and as their human companions, it is the responsibility of every owner to take proper care of their health. At Pet Trust, we strive to ensure that your pets have a healthy and painless life. With our advanced laboratory, state-of-the-art facilities, and specialized veterinarians, we share the same compassion that you have for your pets. So, our veterinarians are up to date with recent studies, clinical trials, and always make sure to provide the most effective treatment for your pets.

Department of Interventional Radiology

Our Interventional Radiography department helps in making the diagnosis, treatment, and recovery of pets easier and comfortable.

Interventional radiography allows performing minimally invasive procedures using real-time imaging and guidance. This is used for both therapy and diagnosis. The instruments used for medical imaging which is used in interventional radiography are x-ray fluoroscopy, computed tomography( CT) scan, magnetic resonance imaging(MRI), and ultrasound. Our vets make small incisions or do the procedure through body orifices.

Department of Diagnostic Imaging

Our advanced diagnostic tools and equipment help us in fast-tracking the treatment of pets.

The department of diagnostic imaging at Pet Trust consists of experienced technicians, modern and sophisticated equipment needed for advanced imaging and diagnosis. These tools help us to collect detailed information about your pet’s health and the procedure for imaging is non-invasive and painless in most cases. These help our doctors create a detailed plan for surgery if required.

Department of Obstetrics and Gynaecology

The department of Obstetrics and Gynecology at Pet Trust aims to help pets have a safe and healthy pregnancy and birth experience.

Our veterinary obstetricians are experts in handling birth complications such as dystocia and cesareans. The gynecologists at Pet Trust use our advanced facilities to diagnose pregnancy and provide preventive treatment for diseases in female pets. We specialize in theriogenology which is managing the reproductive health and fertility in small pets. With advanced care and treatment.
